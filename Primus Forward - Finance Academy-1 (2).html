PCAP (Primus Certified Accounts Professional)

Program Objective: To bridge the gap between academic learning and practical job expectations in the finance domain. This 6-month intensive program is designed to transform students and early professionals into job-ready finance and accounting experts, equipped with real-world skills, technical proficiency, and workplace readiness through a unique blend of classroom instruction and live firm-based training.

Program Structure:

  • Offline Classroom Sessions: Structured curriculum-based learning by domain experts, case-based learning, and role-plays (3 Months, 200 Hours)
  • On-the-Job Practical Training: In-house training within our consulting firm across live projects, client accounts, and internal operations (3 Months, 420 Hours)
  • Total Duration: Holistic job-oriented development (6 Months, 720 Hours)

Key Details:

Duration: 6 Months

Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate (Job-ready)

Timeframe: Intensive, Full-time Commitment

Certification: Primus Certified Accounts Professional

Price: Inquire for Details

Frequently Asked Questions

The PCAP (Primus Certified Accounts Professional) program is an intensive 6-month course, split into 3 months of offline classroom sessions and 3 months of in-firm practical training.
Our program is designed to make you job-ready. We provide extensive practical training, resume building workshops, mock interviews, and guidance on LinkedIn presence to help you secure a role in the finance and accounting industry. The 3-month in-firm training is a direct bridge to real-world job expectations.
The PCAP program is suitable for students who have completed higher education and are looking to enter the finance industry, current professionals seeking to upskill, and individuals at the beginning of their finance careers. While a basic understanding of finance is helpful, the program is comprehensive and designed to build skills from the ground up.
